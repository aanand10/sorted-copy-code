Sort-Ed

An initiative by Students for Students.

Sort-Ed is a platform empowering all students with right opportunities and helping them enhance in all aspects, throughout their educational journey. Sort-Ed aspires in overcoming barriers which hold back students from performing at their highest potential. Sort-Ed acts as a key for students to excel in their career by fulfilling all their educational needs in affordable prices. We at sort-Ed believes in Sustainable progress while holdings hands of mother nature.

Sort-Ed provides better solutions towards educational requirements. Starting with Textbooks subscriptions platform where students pay for platform instead of paying for books. They can get complete set of Semester textbooks delivered at doorstep. Post semester end, students exchange their current book-set with next semester books. A library platform where students can choose from vast collection of novels, fictions, value addition books, self help books and many more. A platform where students can avail for all necessary stationeries they need. All at ease and minimal subscription fees with additional exciting rewards

Our Vision

" To empower every student to excel at their highest potential by providing endless opportunities beholding mother nature."

Our Mission

" To become the most prominent platform for students, helping and supporting them in all their Educational and career-based needs. To make students life sorted by all sustainable means and being major source for green contribution."

Lets Go Green

  A single tree could produce an average of 8000 sheets of paper. considering, a 200 paged textbook, 40 textbooks could be produced by one single tree. As per the fact an engineering student uses around 40-45 textbooks throughout his 4 years journey and majority of the books end up nowhere but at 'raddiwalas' or in trash. Calculating an average of 100 students in one engineering classroom, 100 trees are cut down to fulfill their 4 year textbook needs.

Sort-ed comes with an initiative wherein 100 trees could fulfill 4 year textbook needs of not 100 but a minimum of 300 students by implementing the simple principle of reuse-reduce-recycle in a smart and strategic manner. By result of which sort-ed aspires to drastically decrease the number of trees cut down for producing same textbooks.

Sort-ed provides a subscription based platform which helps students with an alternative for inefficient method of buying, using and throwing textbooks every semester. Instead, Sort-ed offers a smart solution where students can subscribe use & exchange their current semester books with that of next semester's., Creating availability of books for reuse instead of getting thrown. This results in decreased need to buy new books and ultimately helping save paper
